Recode2S - DNA recoding to type II-s parts

This is a tool for recoding DNA to type II-S compatible genetic parts that screens for restriction enzyme recognition sites, recodes them to silent mutations and adds of overhangs according to the Common Syntax and CIDAR/MoClo standards.

Currently, BsaI and SapI are recoded.
